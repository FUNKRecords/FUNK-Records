About Us

X.L.T Records was created to give artists full control over their music — no hidden fees, no delays, no middlemen. We're built from experience, frustration, and a drive to create a better path for independent artists.

Our platform supports genres including Phonk, Lo-fi, Chill, Instrumental, and Electronic. We focus on high-quality releases, transparent royalty reporting, and global distribution to Spotify, Apple Music, TikTok, and more.
